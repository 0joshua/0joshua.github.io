About

I'm a Product Manager, husband and father based in Colorado, USA.

I enjoy spending time with my family, continually learning, hiking, music and more.

Brief Career Summary

I began as a Market Risk Manager at Barclays Investment Bank but longed for more impactful work and to experience the world. This led me to a microfinance institution in Accra, Ghana. I spent just under a year there - handling a "run on the bank" whilst enjoying the culture, country and beer. Late one night I had to withdraw my own cash and head to a branch to (rightfully) pay clients who'd locked staff into the branch, as they couldn't withdraw their money - wild! It was a humbling, eye-opening experience that I would recommend to anyone.

Whilst there, I discovered the emerging off-grid solar industry, trying to solve the problem that more than 1 billion people don't have grid electricity access. I spent an incredible two years as fourth employee at off-grid solar startup MeshPower where I first delved into Product Management (and much more). We connected around 5,000 people to our microgrids and provided them with lighting, phone charging, radios and more

Most recently I was Head of Product at THG Plc, leading a team of 16 Product Managers building a SaaS ecommerce platform that operated at scale. In 2021, it received more than 1 billion visits to sites on the platform with over $2 billion GMV passing through the checkout.
